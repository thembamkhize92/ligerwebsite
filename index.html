About Us

Liger aims to be an all-in-one app that will raise your average order value by showing menu items to consumers intelligently, giving restaurants immediate customer feedback, and most significantly, doing away with the need for menus altogether by using a QR code.

Offering

Increase Revenue

We rearrange and update your menu to encourage clients to purchase more of the higher-yielding items.

Stock Consolidation

To decrease waste, simply initiate a sale on products that are about to expire.

Time Efficiency

Waiting staff saves 5 minutes for every table thanks to our order flow.

Tech Savvy business

The app eliminates the need for menus, which cuts the cost on printing and maintaining the cleanliness of these menus. Which is the future of sit in restaurants.

Direct customer feedback

Customers will be able to rate the service from the restaurant, allowing the restaurants to make quick and congusive improvements.

How it works

Connect to restaurant or malls Wi-Fi

Download the App from Apple store or Google store

Open the App

Scan the restaurants QR Code through the App

Pick your meal & Enjoy!
